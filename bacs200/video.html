Supporting the Vision

The strategies implemented to fulfill the Alzheimer's Association's vision are as follows:

  • Provide care and support for individuals affected by Alzheimer's and other forms of dementia.
  • Advance research towards prevention, treatment, and a cure.
  • Advocate for critical initiatives at the state and federal level for research and care.
