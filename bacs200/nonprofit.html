The Vision

As a volunteer based health organization, The Alzheimer's Association aims to create a world free of Alzheimer's disease and all other forms of dementia. Through volunteers, fundraising events, such as The Walk to End Alzheimer's, and donation programs the Alzheimer's Association is fighting against a disease that affects over 5.8 million Americans.

Supporting the Vision

The strategies implemented to fulfill the Alzheimer's Association's vision are as follows:

  • Provide care and support for individuals affected by Alzheimer's and other forms of dementia.
  • Advance research towards prevention, treatment, and a cure.
  • Advocate for critical initiatives at the state and federal level for research and care.
